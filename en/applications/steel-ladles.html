Steel Ladles

Specialized Refractory Solutions for Steel Ladle Lining Systems

Henan Yuandake provides comprehensive refractory solutions for steel ladles, designed to withstand the demanding conditions of molten steel handling, secondary metallurgy, and steel transport. Our ladle lining systems ensure optimal performance, extended service life, and improved steel quality.

Steel Ladle Challenges

  • Extreme thermal shock during steel tapping
  • Chemical attack from molten steel and slag
  • Mechanical stress from ladle movement and stirring
  • Rapid temperature changes during heating and cooling
  • Erosion from steel flow and argon stirring

Our Steel Ladle Solutions

Working Lining Systems

High-performance working lining designed for direct contact with molten steel, offering superior chemical resistance and thermal shock performance.

  • Magnesia-carbon bricks for sidewalls
  • Alumina-magnesia-carbon for bottom areas
  • Specialized slide gate refractories

Safety Lining

Reliable safety lining systems providing structural integrity and thermal insulation for extended ladle service life.

  • High alumina bricks for permanent lining
  • Insulating bricks for thermal protection
  • Backup insulation materials

Ladle Bottom Systems

Specialized bottom lining systems designed to handle extreme conditions at the ladle bottom with superior erosion resistance.

  • Impact pads for tapping zones
  • Erosion-resistant bottom blocks
  • Argon stirring plug systems

Ladle Zone Applications

Slag Line Zone

Critical area requiring maximum slag resistance and thermal shock protection.

Recommended: Magnesia-Chrome Brick

Sidewall Areas

Primary contact zone with molten steel requiring chemical stability and thermal performance.

Recommended: High Alumina Brick

Bottom Impact Zone

High-stress area requiring maximum impact and erosion resistance.

Recommended: Refractory Castable

Ladle Bottom

Foundation area requiring structural strength and thermal stability.

Recommended: Steel Fiber Castable
