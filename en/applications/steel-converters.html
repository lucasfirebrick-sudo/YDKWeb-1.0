Steel Converters

Comprehensive Refractory Solutions for BOF, AOD, and Steelmaking Converters

Henan Yuandake provides advanced refractory solutions specifically designed for steel converters, including Basic Oxygen Furnaces (BOF), Argon Oxygen Decarburization (AOD) converters, and other steelmaking processes. Our specialized refractories deliver exceptional performance in the demanding environment of modern steelmaking.

Key Challenges in Steel Converters

  • Extreme temperature fluctuations during steel production
  • Severe slag attack and chemical corrosion
  • Mechanical stress from stirring and blowing operations
  • Thermal shock from rapid heating and cooling cycles
  • Erosion from molten metal and slag flow
