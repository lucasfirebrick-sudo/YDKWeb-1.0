Cement Kiln Zone Applications

Burning Zone

Highest temperature zone requiring maximum thermal and chemical resistance.

Recommended: Magnesia Chrome Brick

Transition Zone

High-stress zone with temperature gradients and chemical attack.

Recommended: High Alumina Brick

Calcining Zone

Medium temperature zone with alkali and thermal cycling.

Recommended: Standard High Alumina Brick

Preheating Zone

Lower temperature zone requiring alkali resistance.

Recommended: Clay Brick
