What's the delivery time?

Standard products 7-15 days, customized products 15-30 days. Urgent orders can be processed within 3-5 days.

Wire transfer (T/T), letter of credit (L/C), corporate transfer and other payment methods, 30% advance payment and 70% payment before delivery.

We provide comprehensive customized services including product formula customization, special size design, and on-site technical support.

Free standard product samples (500g-1kg), customized samples charge cost fees, including test reports.

How to get technical support?

24-hour technical hotline, online support, on-site service, technical training and other comprehensive technical support.
