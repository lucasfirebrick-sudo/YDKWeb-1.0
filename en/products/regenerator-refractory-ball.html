Clay Brick Overview

Clay bricks are fundamental refractory materials manufactured from high-quality clay and bauxite. These bricks are characterized by their excellent thermal properties, chemical stability, and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for a wide range of industrial applications.

Manufacturing Process

Our clay bricks are produced using advanced manufacturing techniques:

  • Careful selection of high-grade clay and bauxite raw materials
  • Precise mixing and grinding to achieve optimal particle size distribution
  • High-pressure forming to ensure consistent density
  • Controlled firing at temperatures up to 1400℃
  • Strict quality control throughout the production process

Key Advantages

  • High Refractoriness: Withstands temperatures up to 1750℃
  • Thermal Shock Resistance: Excellent performance under rapid temperature changes
  • Chemical Stability: Resistant to slag and chemical corrosion
  • Cost-Effective: Optimal balance of performance and price
  • Versatility: Suitable for various industrial applications

Quality Assurance

All clay bricks undergo comprehensive testing including:

  • Chemical composition analysis
  • Physical property testing
  • Thermal performance evaluation
  • Mechanical strength assessment
  • Dimensional accuracy verification
