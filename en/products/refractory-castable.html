Refractory Castable Overview

Refractory castables are unshaped refractory materials that can be cast, pumped, or gunned into place. They offer excellent workability and can form complex shapes, making them ideal for modern furnace construction and repair.

Key Advantages

  • Versatile Installation: Can be cast, pumped, or sprayed
  • Complex Shapes: Forms intricate geometries easily
  • Rapid Installation: Faster than brick construction
  • Monolithic Structure: No joints to fail
  • Superior Performance: Excellent thermal and mechanical properties
