Product Overview

Henan Yuandake's Lightweight Silica Brick combines the exceptional acid resistance of high-silica content with superior thermal insulation properties. With ≥90% SiO₂ content and carefully controlled porosity, these bricks provide excellent performance in acidic environments while offering significant energy savings through reduced heat loss.

Our lightweight silica bricks are specifically engineered for applications requiring both acid resistance and thermal insulation. The high silica content ensures excellent chemical stability against acidic conditions, while the lightweight design provides superior insulation performance and reduced structural load.

Installation Guidelines

Installation Process

1

Surface Preparation

Ensure installation surface is chemically compatible and free from alkali contamination that could affect silica brick performance.

2

Mortar Selection

Use high-silica mortar with similar chemical composition. Avoid high-alkali mortars that could cause chemical reactions.

3

Thermal Expansion Consideration

Account for silica brick thermal expansion characteristics, including potential volume changes during heating.

4

Controlled Heat-up

Follow gradual heating schedule: 25°C/hour to 600°C, then 15°C/hour to 1000°C, then normal rate.

Critical Installation Points

Alkali Contamination

Avoid contact with alkali materials during installation as they can cause chemical attack of silica brick.

Critical Temperature Range

Pay special attention during 200-600°C range where silica undergoes phase transformations.

Thermal Expansion

Provide adequate expansion space as silica brick shows permanent expansion during first heating.

Chemical Compatibility

Ensure all adjacent materials are chemically compatible with high-silica content bricks.
