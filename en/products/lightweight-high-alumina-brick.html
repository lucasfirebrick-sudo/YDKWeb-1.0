Product Overview

Henan Yuandake's Lightweight High Alumina Brick represents the pinnacle of refractory insulation technology, combining exceptional thermal properties with superior mechanical strength. With ≥75% Al₂O₃ content and ultra-low density ranging from 0.4-1.3 g/cm³, these bricks deliver outstanding thermal insulation while maintaining excellent structural integrity.

Our lightweight high alumina bricks are engineered using advanced manufacturing techniques, incorporating carefully controlled porosity to achieve optimal balance between insulation performance and mechanical strength. The unique pore structure provides excellent thermal shock resistance and superior energy efficiency.

Installation Guidelines

Installation Process

1

Surface Preparation

Ensure the installation surface is clean, dry, and level. Remove any debris or contaminants that could affect bonding.

2

Mortar Selection

Use high-alumina refractory mortar compatible with the brick grade. Mortar composition should match brick chemistry.

3

Brick Laying

Install bricks with consistent mortar joints (3-5mm). Maintain proper alignment and level throughout installation.

4

Curing Process

Allow 24-48 hours curing time before heat-up. Follow controlled heating schedule to prevent thermal shock.

Installation Tips

Temperature Control

Gradual heat-up at 50°C/hour up to 600°C, then 25°C/hour to operating temperature.

Expansion Joints

Provide adequate expansion joints to accommodate thermal movement during operation.

Quality Inspection

Inspect mortar joints and brick alignment before proceeding to next course of installation.

Safety Measures

Use appropriate PPE and follow safety protocols for handling refractory materials.
