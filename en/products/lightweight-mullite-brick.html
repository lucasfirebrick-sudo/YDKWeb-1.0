Product Overview

Henan Yuandake's Lightweight Mullite Brick represents advanced refractory technology, featuring exceptional thermal shock resistance combined with superior insulation properties. With 70-80% Al₂O₃ content and mullite crystal structure, these bricks deliver outstanding performance in demanding high-temperature applications where thermal cycling is critical.

Our lightweight mullite bricks are manufactured using premium raw materials and advanced forming techniques, creating a controlled pore structure that provides excellent thermal insulation while maintaining mechanical integrity. The unique mullite crystal phase offers exceptional resistance to thermal shock and chemical attack.

Installation Guidelines

Installation Process

1

Substrate Preparation

Ensure substrate is clean, level, and compatible with mullite brick thermal expansion characteristics.

2

Mortar Selection

Use high-alumina mortar with similar thermal expansion properties. Mullite-based mortars recommended for optimal performance.

3

Expansion Joints

Provide adequate expansion joints (5-10mm) to accommodate thermal movement and prevent stress concentration.

4

Heat-up Schedule

Follow controlled heating schedule: 25°C/hour to 300°C, then 50°C/hour to operating temperature.

Critical Installation Points

Thermal Expansion

Account for differential thermal expansion between mullite brick and adjacent materials.

Controlled Heat-up

Never exceed 100°C/hour heating rate to prevent thermal shock damage during commissioning.

Mortar Compatibility

Ensure mortar thermal properties match brick characteristics to prevent differential movement.

Handling Care

Handle with care during installation - avoid dropping or impact that could create micro-cracks.
