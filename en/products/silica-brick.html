Silica Brick Overview

Silica bricks are high-quality acidic refractory materials containing at least 96% SiO₂. They are characterized by excellent thermal stability, high load-bearing capacity at elevated temperatures, and superior resistance to acidic slags and atmospheres.

Mineralogical Composition

Our silica bricks undergo controlled phase transformation during production:

  • Cristobalite: Primary phase providing thermal stability
  • Tridymite: Secondary phase enhancing thermal properties
  • Quartz: Residual phase from raw materials
  • Glass Phase: Binding matrix ensuring structural integrity

Manufacturing Process

Our silica bricks are manufactured using specialized technology:

  • Selection of high-purity quartzite and quartz sand
  • Careful grinding to achieve optimal particle size distribution
  • Addition of mineralizers to promote phase transformation
  • High-pressure molding for consistent density
  • Controlled firing with precise temperature profile (1450-1480℃)
  • Gradual cooling to develop desired crystal structure

Key Performance Features

  • High Refractoriness: Service temperatures up to 1650℃
  • Thermal Stability: Excellent dimensional stability at high temperatures
  • Load Bearing: Maintains strength under load at elevated temperatures
  • Chemical Resistance: Superior resistance to acidic slags and gases
  • Low Thermal Expansion: Minimal volume changes during heating
  • Good Spalling Resistance: Withstands thermal cycling

Quality Assurance

Each batch undergoes comprehensive testing including:

  • X-ray diffraction for phase composition analysis
  • Chemical analysis for purity verification
  • Physical property testing (porosity, density, strength)
  • Thermal behavior evaluation
  • Microstructure examination
  • Service performance simulation

Handling and Installation

Important considerations for optimal performance:

  • Gradual heating to avoid thermal shock
  • Proper joint design to accommodate expansion
  • Compatible mortar selection
  • Controlled cooling during maintenance
