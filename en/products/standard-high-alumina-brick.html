Product Overview

Henan Yuandake's Standard High Alumina Brick represents reliable, cost-effective refractory solutions for demanding industrial applications. With 55-75% Al₂O₃ content and dense structure, these bricks provide excellent mechanical strength, superior thermal stability, and proven performance across various high-temperature industries.

Our standard high alumina bricks are manufactured using carefully selected raw materials and optimized firing processes, ensuring consistent quality and reliable performance. The dense structure provides excellent resistance to mechanical stress and thermal shock while maintaining cost-effectiveness for large-scale applications.

Installation Guidelines

Installation Process

1

Foundation Preparation

Ensure foundation is level, clean, and capable of supporting the structural load of dense refractory lining.

2

Mortar Application

Apply high-alumina mortar evenly. Maintain consistent joint thickness of 3-5mm for optimal structural integrity.

3

Brick Laying

Install bricks in proper pattern with tight joints. Check alignment and level regularly during installation.

4

Curing and Heat-up

Allow proper curing time and follow gradual heat-up schedule to achieve optimal performance.
