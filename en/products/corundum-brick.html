Corundum Brick Overview

Corundum bricks represent the pinnacle of refractory technology, featuring ≥90% Al₂O₃ content and exceptional refractoriness ≥1900℃. These premium-grade bricks deliver outstanding performance in the most demanding high-temperature applications where maximum thermal and chemical resistance is required.

Manufacturing Process

Our corundum bricks are produced using advanced manufacturing techniques:

  • Selection of high-purity fused or sintered corundum aggregates
  • Precision mixing with ultra-pure binding materials
  • High-pressure molding to achieve optimal density
  • Controlled firing at temperatures up to 1700℃
  • Rigorous quality control throughout the production process

Key Advantages

  • Extreme Refractoriness: ≥1900℃ service temperature for most demanding applications
  • Superior Mechanical Strength: Exceptional strength at high temperatures
  • Chemical Resistance: Excellent resistance to chemical attack and corrosion
  • Wear Resistance: Outstanding abrasion resistance in severe conditions
  • Premium Quality: Highest grade refractory for critical applications
  • Thermal Stability: Minimal dimensional changes at operating temperatures

Quality Assurance

All corundum bricks undergo comprehensive testing including:

  • Chemical composition analysis
  • Physical property testing (density, porosity)
  • High-temperature mechanical strength evaluation
  • Refractoriness under load testing
  • Dimensional accuracy verification
  • Corrosion resistance assessment
