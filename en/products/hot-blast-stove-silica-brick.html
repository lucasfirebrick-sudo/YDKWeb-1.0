Hot Blast Stove Silica Brick Overview

Henan Yuandake's Hot Blast Stove Silica Brick is specifically designed for steel industry blast furnace applications, providing exceptional thermal stability and chemical resistance in demanding high-temperature environments. With ≥96% SiO₂ content, these specialized bricks ensure reliable performance in critical steel production processes.

Key Advantages

  • High SiO₂ Content: ≥96% SiO₂ for superior thermal properties
  • Thermal Stability: Excellent performance up to 1650℃
  • Chemical Resistance: Resistant to acidic slag corrosion

  • Long Service Life: Extended operational periods in blast furnaces
