Semi-Silica Brick Overview

Semi-silica bricks are intermediate-grade refractory materials with 65-85% SiO₂ content, offering an optimal balance between silica and alumina properties. These bricks deliver versatile performance for intermediate temperature applications while maintaining cost-effectiveness for diverse industrial needs.

Manufacturing Process

Our semi-silica bricks are produced using advanced manufacturing techniques:

  • Careful selection of high-quality silica and clay materials
  • Precise blending to achieve optimal SiO₂/Al₂O₃ ratio
  • High-pressure molding for consistent density and strength
  • Controlled firing at temperatures up to 1350℃
  • Strict quality control throughout the production process

Key Advantages

  • Balanced Composition: Optimal silica-alumina ratio for versatile performance
  • Good Thermal Properties: Suitable for intermediate temperature ranges up to 1600℃
  • Chemical Stability: Resistant to acidic and neutral slags
  • Cost Effective: Economic solution for general industrial applications
  • Versatile Applications: Wide range of industrial uses

Quality Assurance

All semi-silica bricks undergo comprehensive testing including:

  • Chemical composition analysis
  • Physical property testing
  • Thermal performance evaluation
  • Mechanical strength assessment
  • Dimensional accuracy verification
